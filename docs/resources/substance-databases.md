## ‚≠ê Essential Resources

### Erowid

**URL:** [erowid.org](https://www.erowid.org/)

The gold standard for substance information since 1995. Comprehensive, peer-trusted, and
non-judgmental.

**Best for:** Detailed substance information, experience reports, harm reduction.

### PsychonautWiki

**URL:** [psychonautwiki.org](https://psychonautwiki.org/)

Community-driven encyclopedia with dosing, pharmacology, and subjective effects.

**Best for:** Dosing information, pharmacology, subjective effects.

### TripSit

**URL:** [tripsit.me](https://tripsit.me/)

Real-time harm reduction chat support, factsheets, and drug interaction checker.

**Best for:** Quick reference, drug interactions, real-time support.
